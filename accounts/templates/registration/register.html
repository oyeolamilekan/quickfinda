What kind of ads would you not accept on rifqoh?

  1. We will reject ads that we consider to be ethically or morally questionable in one way or the other.

  2. We reject ads for HYIPs and most MLMs because they are fundamentally unsustainable.

  3. We reject ads for health remedies that make claims that have not been verified by NAFDAC because we know that they don't work as advertised.
